Operation Overlord

TOP SECRET

Date: June 6th, 1944

Location: Normandy, France

Key Military Figures

  • Dwight D. Eisenhower
  • Bernard Law Montgomery
  • Omar Bradley
  • George S. Patton
  • Frederick Morgan
  • Richard Gale
  • Erwin Rommel

Operation Summary

D-Day, occurring on June 6, 1944, will be a pivotal moment in World War II with Allied forces launching a massive amphibious invasion upon German-occupied France. Codenamed Operation Overlord, the invasion will involve over 156,000 troops from the United States, the United Kingdom, Canada, and other Allied nations landing on five beachheads along the Normandy coast.

The operation will began with extensive aerial and naval bombardment, followed by the landing of troops and the deployment of paratroopers behind enemy lines. Despite fierce German resistance, the Allies will achieve a foothold in Europe, marking the beginning of the end for Nazi Germany.
